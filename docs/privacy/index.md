# AI Token Watch Privacy Policy

[简体中文](./zh-CN/)

Effective Date: July 4, 2026

AI Token Watch is a local-only macOS app for summarizing token usage from coding-agent records on your Mac.

## Data Collection

AI Token Watch does not collect, transmit, sell, share, or upload user data.

AI Token Watch does not require an account or login. It does not include analytics, advertising, telemetry, tracking, or third-party SDKs.

## Local File Access

AI Token Watch reads local Claude Code, Codex, and opencode usage files only after you grant access through the macOS file picker. All parsing, aggregation, and cost estimation happen locally on your device.

The app may display local information derived from those files, such as token counts, model names, session identifiers, and project paths. This information remains on your device.

## Local Storage

AI Token Watch stores app preferences and security-scoped bookmarks in local UserDefaults so it can remember settings and restore access to folders you have authorized. This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

## Network Access

AI Token Watch itself does not access the network. The Privacy Policy entry opens this public policy page in your default browser.

## Contact

For privacy questions, use the support contact listed on the App Store product page or open an issue at:
